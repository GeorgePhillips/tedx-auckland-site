TEDxAuckland

Our mission at TEDxAuckland is to make one of the Pacific's leading platforms of great ideas, story telling, creativity, and actionable innovation. To create and foster a sense of community amongst people and organisations who have gathered (and continue to gather) around this event and to provide a cultural focus and impetus for conversation and debate. Our 2017 event is on December 1-2 at the Aotea Centre - Join the Facebook event - Over two days, you'll experience over 24 incredible speakers and performers from around the world. Tickets are still available and include food and beverages for both days.
