#TEDxYouthAuckland
TEDXYOUTH@Auckland 2018 | FRIDAY MAY 4TH, 2018 | AOTEA SQUARE

Youth

TEDxYouth@Auckland was last held in 2013 however, due to popular demand, the team that produces TEDxAuckland have brought back the youth-focused event in 2018.

Join 2,000 other young people (14-21 years) for a day of talks from inspiring young people from New Zealand and around the world. The event will spill out of the theatre and into Aotea Square, where event partners will be hosting activations and spreading their ideas worth sharing.

Please follow the TEDxAuckland Facebook page and join the TEDxYouth@Auckland 2018 Facebook event to be in the know with event updates and speaker announcements.
